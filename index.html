About Gypsy Grill

Gypsy Grill is your destination for the finest Middle Eastern dishes. From mouth-watering shawarma to flavorful falafel, our chefs craft each dish with expertise and love.

Join us for an unforgettable culinary journey through the heart of the Middle East.

Our Menu

Shawarma

Delicious spiced meat, perfectly roasted and wrapped in warm pita bread.

Falafel

Crispy on the outside, soft on the inside. Served with tahini sauce.
